## Hello World 🌍

Welcome to **BlueTricks** — my personal corner of the internet dedicated to **Cyber Security**, **Red Teaming**, and **CTF Writeups**.

I'm **Muhammad Ghareeb** (aka **0xmureeb**), a Security Engineer based in Egypt. I've been passionate about offensive security for years, and I finally decided to document my journey, share techniques, and give back to the community.

## What to Expect

Here's what you'll find on this blog:

- 🔴 **Red Team Operations** — Tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) used in real-world engagements
- 🏴 **CTF Writeups** — Step-by-step solutions for challenges from HackTheBox, TryHackMe, and competitions
- 🛡️ **Security Research** — Vulnerability analysis, exploit development, and tool reviews
- 📚 **Tutorials** — Getting started guides for beginners in offensive security

## Why "BlueTricks"?

Because even red teamers need to understand the blue side. Knowing how defenders think makes you a better attacker. This blog bridges both worlds.

## Stay Connected

Follow along on [GitHub](https://github.com/0mureeb) — and feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate or have questions about any post.

Let's hack the planet. 🚀
